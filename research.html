Research & Pre-prints

My theoretical work focuses on Riemannian metrics, geometric analysis, and flow equations on smooth manifolds.

Convergence of Ricci Flow on Compact Manifolds

Pre-print

Investigating the singularity models and long-time behavior of the Ricci flow equation:

$$ \frac{\partial}{\partial t} g_{ij} = -2 R_{ij} $$

This paper examines structural limits when the scalar curvature remains bounded.

Classification of Gradient Ricci Solitons

Published

A systematic exploration of steady and expanding gradient Ricci solitons satisfying the equation:

$$ R_{ij} + \nabla_i \nabla_j f = \lambda g_{ij} $$

We provide new rigidity results for dimension \( n \geq 4 \) under specific curvature assumptions.
