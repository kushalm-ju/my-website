Spacetime Metric Visualizer

A web-based tool for plotting geodesics around simulated mass distributions. Calculates geodesic equations dynamically using Runge-Kutta (RK4) integration:

$$ \frac{d^2 x^\mu}{d\tau^2} + \Gamma^\mu_{\alpha\beta} \frac{dx^\alpha}{d\tau} \frac{dx^\beta}{d\tau} = 0 $$
